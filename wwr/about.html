Rocky River Rafting

Welcome to Rocky River Rafting. Our goal is to provide safe and exciting rafting experiences that connect people with nature. We aim to create lasting memories of adventure and friends.

History

Established in 2010 by a group of adventure lovers in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Rocky River Rafting started small with just a few rafts. Over time, our company has grown into a top-notch adventure spot, known for thrilling river trips. Committed to safety and the environment, we offer exciting experiences on Argentina's most beautiful rivers. Our friendly guides are always coming up with new ideas to make your trip better. Today, Rocky River Rafting is a leader in adventure tourism, welcoming travelers from all over to join us for exciting journeys through nature's wonders.
