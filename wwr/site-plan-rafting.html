Overview

Purpose

Experience the thrill of river rafting with us! Our website offers unforgettable adventures, educational resources, and customer reviews. Book now for an exciting journey with your friends.

Audience

Scenario Questions and Answers:

Who are the target customers? Describe their life situation.
Our target customers include people who love nature and adventure, families looking for fun activities, friends planning group outings, and organizations interested in learning and team-building. They come from different backgrounds and ages, but they all enjoy outdoor experiences.

What do they want?
They want fun and memorable experiences that mix adventure with learning, teamwork, and personal growth. Adventure lovers seek excitement in beautiful natural settings, families and friends want activities that everyone can enjoy together, and educational groups want safe and educational introductions to rafting.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
They expect to find clear information about our rafting trips, including details about the rivers we go on, safety tips, how to book, and educational opportunities. They might want to know about trip schedules, what to bring, and how rafting can meet their specific needs and interests.
